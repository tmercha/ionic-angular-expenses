Expense Recorder Expense Recorder About This App This app lets you record and save information to track your expenses. Click on 'New Expense' and add the required information to get started. When you take a photo of your reciept, the app will automatically record when the photo was taken for future reference. Go to 'My Expenses' to see all the expenses you have recorded. Click on each one to see more details, or swipe left to delete. A running total of your expenses will be displayed at the top.
